cumulo

The simple solution for backing up, sharing and saving all of your files, from pictures to tax documents, in one easy to access place. It is cross platform and supports Apple products, Android products, Windows devices and even your gaming consoles. Everything is stored in one cloud that you can access from any of the devices on your network.

Made in collaboration with: Jarret Pickens

cumulo Features

Connect

Cumulo is a device that plugs directly into your wireless router to access your network and connect to the cumulo cloud.

Backup

By using the companion app on your phone or tablet you can quickly select which devices you would like to add to your cumulo cloud and they will automatically back-up according to settings of your choosing.

Share

You can access files on one device from another device and share them without having to physically transfer them by emailing or messaging them to yourself.
