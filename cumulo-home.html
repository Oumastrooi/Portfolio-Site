cumulo

The simple solution for backing up, sharing and saving all of your files, from pictures to tax documents, in one easy to access place. It is cross platform and supports Apple products, Android products, Windows devices and even your gaming consoles. Everything is stored in one cloud that you can access from any of the devices on your network.

Simple • Safe • Secure

By using the companion app on your phone or tablet you can quickly select which devices already on your network that you would like to add to your cumulo cloud and they will automatically back-up according to a few settings of your choosing. You can also access files on one device from another device without having to physically transfer them by emailing or messaging them to yourself.

Compatible Devices

cumulo is compatible across all platforms and smart devices
