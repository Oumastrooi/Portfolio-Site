A Little About Me

My name is Elize Geldenhuys and I am from South Africa. My family and I moved to the United States in 2001 and then to Texas in 2008. We go back to visit our family as often as we can but I can’t imagine leaving Texas any time soon. I’ve already gotten into the habit of saying y’all so there’s no going back now!

When I’m not in front of my computer working, I’m outside camping (not glamping) or hanging out at the beach with my family.

I’ll never turn down an invitation for coffee or miss a chance to cook a delicious meal.

Get in touch if you need a coffee companion, want to talk about traveling the world, have an obsession with new technology or would like to talk about my work. Any pointers or critiques are always appreciated!
