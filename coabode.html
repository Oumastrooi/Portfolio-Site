About the Client
Who They Are

coabode is an online community that is focused on bringing single mothers together for the benefits of housesharing. They have made it their mission to provide resources that single mothers may need to overcome the financial and emotional hardships that they face.

Project Objectives

coabode has a strong mission and a successful history of matching women for housesharing. We focused on creating, unifying and strengthening the brand across all facets of coabode.

Challenges & Strategies

Pain Points

The website is uninviting, unprofessional and looks untrustworthy.

Solutions

We updated the website to feel more inviting and provide more information. We focused on making it easy to use so that it doesn't take much time to set up a profile and get started.

The process that coabode currently uses to match single mothers is time consuming and relies heavily on the mothers reading through the bio sections of other mothers in their area.

The questionnaire section of the coabode website takes into account important aspects of the womens lives and condenses it into easy to sort numerical matching so that the women don't have to read through 10 pages looking for the perfect match.

Not all women who go to coabode are interested in housesharing. Many are just looking for emotional support and coabode does not currently create casual events just to meet other single mothers.

The coabode app is an easy way to stay connected to the social coabode community without having to commit to housesharing. It provides financial and emotional resources, and a tether to the community with local events for mothers and their children.

App Design

coabode is there for you for when you have already found someone to share a house with or when you are just looking for a coabode event in your area. Events like playdates, events at the park or a quick coffee are all available and easy to attend.

coabode also offers articles that are curated by single mothers, for single mothers. The article categories include finance, child raising, coupons and even DIY so you can learn some quick fixes for things around the house to save you money.

Website Design

The coabode website has been completely overhauled to increase ease of access. It provides more information about the matches provided as well as using an algorithm to calculate the best matches possible.

The questionnaire has been shortened so that the users can spend less time filling out their information to save time. The algorithm also lets them see their matches instantly without having to wade through profiles looking at each one.
