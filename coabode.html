About the Client
Who They Are

coabode is an online community that is focused on bringing single mothers together for the benefits of housesharing. They have made it their mission to make sure that these women have the resources they need to overcome the financial and emotional hardships that are often associated with being a single mother.

Project Objectives

coabode has a strong mission and a successful history of matching women for housesharing. We focused on creating, unifying and strengthening the brand across all facets of coabode.

Challenges & Strategies

Pain Points

The website is uninviting, unprofessional and looks untrustworthy.

Solutions

We updated the website to feel more inviting and provide more information. We focused on making it easy to use so that it doesn't take much time to set up a profile and get started.

The process that coabode currently uses to match single mothers is time consuming and relies heavily on the mothers reading through the bio sections of other mothers in their area.

The questionnaire section of the coabode website takes into account important aspects of the womens lives and condenses it into easy to sort numerical matching so that the women don't have to read through 10 pages looking for the perfect match.

Not all women who go to coabode are interested in housesharing. Many are just looking for emotional support and coabode does not currently create casual events just to meet other single mothers.

The coabode app is an easy way to stay connected to the social coabode community without having to commit to housesharing. It provides financial and emotional resources, and a tether to the community with local events for mothers and their children.

The Terra Device

Terra is designed to help navigate your trail, tag your favorite locations, view your progress and help in emergency situations. Eight LED lights and a physical button help conserve battery life. Terra is waterproof and uses a watch battery so you never have to charge it and can easily change the battery when it needs to be replaced. Your phone doesn’t need to be connected for the device to work, so your phone battery remains unaffected during your adventures.

Process Work

Device Functionality

Emergency

Push and hold the button in the center to send out an emergency GPS signal. Red lights flash so that you can be sure your request for help has been sent. Pushing the button again turns off the beacon. Green lights will flash three times to confirm the cancellation.

Navigation

A single push of the button will display a red light that points North and a green light that tells you in which direction you need to walk to either get back to your trail or to stay on it. The lights stay on for a few seconds then automatically turn off so that the battery is not wasted.

Progress

Double tapping the center button will show you how much of your trail you have completed. Green lights light up in a clockwise rotation starting from the top of the device with each light equaling 20% of your current trail.

Geotag

Tripple tapping the button geotags your current location as a point of interest along your trail. You can later revisit this location from the app or microsite to add pictures or comments that you can share with the Terra community. Blue lights flash in an alternating pattern so you can be sure that that your location has been saved.
